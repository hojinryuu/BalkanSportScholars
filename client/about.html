Our Mission

At BalkanSportsScholars, our mission is to pave the path for
talented athletes to secure athletic scholarships that enable them
to pursue their sports and education simultaneously at prestigious
universities and colleges in the United States.

At BalkanSportsScholars, we are a team of current and former
student-athletes who have walked the very path we now guide others
along. Our founders come from the Balkan region and having excelled at
the collegiate level in the United States, bring invaluable firsthand experience.

We understand the challenges of playing overseas and have embraced
the opportunities, thriving both athletically and academically. Our mission
is to share this knowledge and support with the next generation of Balkan
athletes, helping them to achieve their dreams.
